At 10:42 PM, responding officers arrived at the Ashdown Hotel after a private dining guest reported Adrian Vale unconscious in the service corridor adjoining the west banquet hall.

Vale had left the dinner table roughly twenty minutes earlier after receiving what one witness called "a short note folded into a matchbook." Staff reported no disturbance, and none of the confirmed guests admitted following him immediately.

A partial timeline places Mara Voss in the dining hall, Dr. Ellis Wren in the smoking room, and hotel operations manager Rohan Bell near the service stairwell. None of those positions remain corroborated across every statement.

Police note: the dinner guest list accounts for all registered attendees, but the front desk supervisor mentioned a private suite booking under a generic hospitality alias tied to the same evening. The document was not included in the first evidence bundle because Bell described it as unrelated routine usage.
