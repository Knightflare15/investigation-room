I was stationed between the hall entrance and the service desk because Mr. Bell asked me to keep delivery staff away from the private corridor. Around 10:15, I saw Mr. Vale step away from the dining room, looking angry and reading something from his phone.

A woman in a dark green coat crossed the far end of the corridor shortly after. I did not know her name then. Later, when detectives showed me three photographs, I thought the third picture might have been her, though I wasn't certain.

What I do remember clearly is Bell snapping at the porter about an "Ashdown Suite" key envelope. That phrase stood out because I had never heard anyone use it during an event shift before.
